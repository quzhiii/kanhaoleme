# Demo Script

Date: 2026-07-02

This is a 3-minute review path for Genesis hackathon judges.

## 0:00 - Open Homepage

Open the demo URL and start on the homepage.

Notice:

- XiaoKan visual identity.
- “Real experience, not a doctor” positioning.
- Entry points for finding experiences and sharing an experience.
- Demo stats: 18 demo experiences, 8 trust labels, 3 city placeholders.

## 0:30 - Open XiaoKan Guide

Click “让小看帮我找经历”.

Try the query:

```text
我想了解减重门诊
```

In the current demo, use the quick query button if text input is not available yet.

Notice:

- XiaoKan is positioned as an AI community guide, not a doctor.
- Results are experience cards, not medical answers.
- The page keeps safety boundary copy visible.

## 1:00 - Review Recommended Experiences

Open a recommended experience card.

Notice:

- Topic, city, and stage.
- TrustBadge labels such as user story, AI summary, and cost clue.
- Cost information is framed as historical clue, not a promise.

## 1:30 - Open Experience Detail

Review the detail page.

Notice:

- AI-organized summary.
- Real-process timeline.
- Questions the user can ask a formal provider.
- Source note and safety notice.

## 2:10 - Open Share Experience

Click “分享经历”.

Fill demo fields such as:

- Topic: 减重管理.
- City: 杭州.
- Stage: 就诊前.
- Process: “我想记录挂号、等待、沟通和复诊安排。”
- Cost: “暂不公开费用。”
- Private info: “手机号、住址、完整病历不公开。”

Notice:

- The page only generates a local preview.
- The current demo does not submit, save, or publish real content.

## 2:40 - Open Safety Boundary

Open “合规边界”.

Notice:

- No diagnosis.
- No treatment recommendations.
- No drug recommendations.
- No doctor or institution ranking.
- No cost promises.
- XiaoKan does not impersonate a doctor or patient.

## 3:00 - Summary

Kanhaoleme demonstrates a safer product direction: real experience cards plus AI organization plus trust labels. The demo validates the interaction model before introducing real user data, backend APIs, moderation, or real AI Agent workflows.
